# Attention Decision Template

Use this template when PEGO decides how the person should handle an event, media item, or leisure opportunity.

## Date

Date and window.

## Context

Current obligations, energy, protected time, directive queue, and attention state.

## Event

Event or media item being decided.

## Options Considered

Watch live, multitask live, highlights later, score only, defer, skip, or escalate.

## Selected Directive

The concrete attention directive.

## Rationale

Why this attention choice is best.

## Tradeoffs

Enjoyment, recovery, social/cultural value, opportunity cost, focus cost, and timing.

## Follow-Up

What PEGO should do after the event or instead of the event.

## Governance Status

Authority, protected-time, obligation, and risk status.

## Review Question

What should PEGO ask afterward?
