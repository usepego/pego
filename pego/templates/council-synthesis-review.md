# Council Synthesis Review

Use this template to evaluate whether the PEGO Council selected, deferred,
escalated, or requested information well.

## Date

Date.

## Source Council Decision

Path or identifier of the council decision being reviewed.

## Source Recommendations

Agent recommendations considered by the council.

## Related Command Response

Command response issued to the human, if any.

## Related Outcome

Directive outcome or session review used as evidence.

## Decision Frame

The concrete decision or directive the council reconciled.

## Selected Outcome

Adopt / Revise / Request more information / Escalate / Block.

## Selected Directive or Question

What the council chose to issue or ask.

## Deferrals

What the council intentionally deferred.

## Outcome Summary

What happened after the selected directive or question.

## Selection Quality

Did the council choose the right domain, timing, altitude, and scope?

## Dissent Handling

Was dissent preserved, ignored, over-weighted, or under-weighted?

## Information Timing

Did the council ask for information too early, too late, unnecessarily, or at
the right time?

## Human Burden

Did the council reduce or increase anxiety, ambiguity, cognitive load, or
execution friction?

## Governance Fit

Did the council respect authority, privacy, reversibility, protected time, and
stakeholder constraints?

## Review Outcome

Improve / Keep / Downgrade / Quarantine / Escalate.

## Future Adjustment

How should future council synthesis change?

## Durable Memory Candidate

What, if anything, should become a context update, operating-register item,
council weighting note, or governance note?

## Next Review

When or under what condition this review should be revisited.
