# Current State Template

This template captures the resources and constraints PEGO can use to move from long-range outcomes to immediate action.

## Location

Where does this person live today?

PEGO may later assess whether the location helps or hurts specific goals. Onboarding should capture facts before asking the person to make strategic judgments.

## Household and Relationships

Who is directly affected by PEGO directives?

What outcomes for those people should PEGO protect or support?

## Work

Current job, responsibilities, schedule, compensation, risks, upside, and constraints.

## Financial Position

Assets, liabilities, income, spending, runway, insurance, obligations, and liquidity.

## Skills

Current skills, proof of skill, market value, and missing capabilities.

## Network

People, communities, institutions, and weak ties that could affect goals.

## Health

Baseline health, sleep, exercise, food, medical constraints, energy, and known risks.

## Calendar and Time

Available hours, recurring commitments, protected time, and scheduling constraints.

## Tools and Assets

Software, accounts, equipment, property, credentials, documents, and systems.

## Constraints

Hard limits, soft limits, obligations, risks, and dependencies.

## Leverage Points

What current resource could produce outsized progress if used well?
