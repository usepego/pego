# Daily Directive Template

## Date

Directive date.

## Operating Thesis

One or two sentences explaining what today is for.

The thesis should connect the day to active goals without overloading the day.

## Governance Status

Authority level, review status, and any conditions.

Default: Level 1, Recommend, unless the private constitution grants stronger authority and governance review approves it.

## Protected Time

Time, relationships, sleep, recovery, and commitments PEGO must not disturb.

## Must Do

The smallest set of directives that should happen today.

Default limit: one primary directive plus critical maintenance.

## Should Do

Useful directives if time and energy allow.

## Optional

Low-stakes actions that may be skipped without failure.

## Health Defaults

Food, movement, sleep, and recovery defaults for the day.

## Work Focus

The primary work or career focus.

## Finance / Admin

Any low-risk finance, planning, or administrative task.

Do not include trading, major spending, debt changes, job-risking decisions, or tax actions without the required review.

## Stop Conditions

Conditions that halt, revise, or escalate the directive.

## End-of-Day Review

- What happened?
- What failed?
- What created friction?
- What produced energy or clarity?
- What should PEGO change tomorrow?
