# Decision Packet Template

Use this template for Level 4 escalations and any high-impact decision.

Decision packets are not daily directives. They are structured governance artifacts for actions that could materially affect finances, health, career, relationships, privacy, legal status, location, household stability, or long-term optionality.

Structured runtimes should preserve the public schema at:

```text
pego/schemas/decision-packet.schema.json
```

## Decision

What decision is being considered?

## Recommendation

Approve / Approve with constraints / Delay / Reject / Request more information / Escalate to outside professional review.

## Authority Level

Usually Level 4: Escalate.

## Agents Consulted

List agents and their positions.

## Constitutional Fit

How does this align or conflict with the constitution?

## Goal Alignment

Which goals does this advance?

Which goals might it harm?

## Current-State Fit

Does the decision fit current resources, constraints, time, energy, location, obligations, skills, assets, and relationships?

## Base Case

What is the most likely outcome?

## Conservative Case

What happens if assumptions are worse than expected?

## Upside Case

What happens if the decision works unusually well?

## Stress Case

What happens under a serious adverse event?

## Expected Benefit

What improves, and why does it matter?

## Costs and Opportunity Costs

What is consumed, delayed, or made unavailable?

## Risks

Classify financial, health, relationship, career, legal, tax, privacy, reputation, time, energy, psychological, and opportunity-cost risks.

## Reversibility

What can be undone?

What cannot be undone?

How long would recovery take if the decision is wrong?

## Privacy Impact

What private information would be used, disclosed, shared with tools, or exposed to third parties?

## Stakeholder Impact

Who else is materially affected?

How are their happiness, agency, stability, time, and disturbance protected?

## Dissent

Include the strongest dissenting view from relevant agents.

Do not flatten real disagreement.

## Waiting Period

Recommended delay before action, if any.

## Required Reviews

Human approval / Governance review / Finance review / Medical review / Legal review / Tax review / Relationship or household discussion.

## Decision Conditions

What must be true before execution?

## Stop Conditions

What should cause PEGO to stop, reverse, or revisit this decision?

## Audit Record

Date, decision owner, agents involved, evidence used, approval status, and next review date.
