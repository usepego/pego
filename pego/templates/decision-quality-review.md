# Decision Quality Review

Use this template to evaluate whether a PEGO directive was a good decision, not
only whether it was completed.

Structured implementations should emit:

```text
pego/schemas/decision-quality-review.schema.json
```

## Date

Date.

## Source Outcome

Directive outcome or review being evaluated.

## Directive

Directive reviewed.

## Completion Class

Completed / Partially completed / Not completed / Blocked / Canceled / Unknown.

## Baseline Comparison

Unmanaged prioritization / Generic assistant advice / To-do list plan /
Single-domain recommendation / Council without goal reconciliation / Council
with goal reconciliation / Unknown.

## Human Burden

Questions asked, time or emotional burden, repeated unanswered questions, and
whether the information request changed the directive.

## Overall Assessment

Improved decision quality / Mixed / Poor fit / Insufficient evidence.

## Next Architecture Adjustment

What should change in agent recommendations, council synthesis, goal
reconciliation, information requests, directive phrasing, or outcome capture?

## Review Notes

Facts separated from interpretation.
