# Directive Outcome Template

Use this template after a directive or synthesized day plan is attempted.

Outcome records are evidence for future PEGO directives. They should be specific enough to change future behavior.

## Date

Date.

## Source Directive

Path or reference to the directive, plan, or decision packet.

## Directive Summary

What PEGO directed or recommended.

## Completion

Completed / Partially completed / Not completed / Blocked / Canceled.

## What Happened

Brief factual account.

## Evidence

Direct observation, human report, telemetry, artifact produced, calendar record, financial model output, or other evidence.

## Friction

What made execution harder?

## Benefit

What improved?

## Outcome Progress

What intended outcome became more real or better understood?

## Contentment Signal

Did the human report more contentment, less contentment, or no material change?

## Cost

What did it consume, disrupt, or delay?

## Protected-Time Impact

None / Low / Medium / High.

## Stakeholder Impact

Any effect on spouse, partner, family, household, coworkers, or other protected stakeholders.

## Environment Impact

Any change to home, workspace, supplies, food defaults, yard, garden, or surrounding environment.

## Follow-Up Directive Candidates

What should PEGO consider next?

## Agent Updates

Which agents should update their model?

## Governance Notes

Authority issue, privacy issue, escalation, stop condition, or review required.

## Next Review

When should this outcome be reviewed again?
