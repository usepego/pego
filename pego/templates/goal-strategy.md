# Goal Strategy Template

Use this template for each major long-range goal.

## Outcome

What result should exist years from now?

## Why It Matters

How is this expected to improve happiness or reduce suffering?

## Time Horizon

Estimated horizon and meaningful review dates.

PEGO should propose the horizon. The person may state urgency or preference, but should not be required to solve the timeline.

## Current Baseline

Where is the person now relative to the outcome?

## Strategic Thesis

What path currently seems most likely to work?

PEGO owns this analysis. The person may supply preferences, constraints, and intuitions, but PEGO should assess strategy, probability, opportunity cost, fallback plans, and alternative paths.

## Milestones

Intermediate outcomes that would show progress.

## Required Capabilities

Skills, assets, relationships, credentials, habits, or health states needed.

## Current Resources

What already exists that PEGO can use?

## First Projects

Near-term projects that build toward the milestones.

## Immediate Actions

Actions the person can take now or this week.

## Fallback Plans

What should PEGO do if the main strategy fails?

## Alternative Paths

Other plausible routes to the same outcome.

## Risks

What could make this goal harmful, impossible, or not worth pursuing?

## Review Cadence

How often should PEGO revisit this strategy?
