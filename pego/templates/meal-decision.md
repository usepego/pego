# Meal Decision Template

Use this template when PEGO chooses what the person should eat now or next.

## Date

Date and meal window.

## Current Context

Hunger, energy, location, schedule, protected time, recent meals, and relevant constraints.

## Goal Context

Current health goal and nutrition strategy.

## Available Options

Food options considered.

Use `pego/templates/food-option.md` for detailed option records when needed.

## Selected Directive

The concrete food directive.

## Rationale

Why this option is best for the current context.

## Tradeoffs

Cost, time, enjoyment, satiety, nutrition, and goal tradeoffs.

## Rejected Options

Options not selected and why.

## Follow-Up

What PEGO should do next: grocery reset, lighter dinner, walk, water, tomorrow breakfast default, or no correction needed.

## Governance Status

Authority, medical, privacy, and evidence status.

## Stop Condition

What should cause PEGO to revise or escalate?

## Review Question

What should be asked after the meal?
