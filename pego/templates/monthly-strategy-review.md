# Monthly Strategy Review Template

Structured runtimes should preserve the public schema at:

```text
pego/schemas/monthly-strategy-review.schema.json
```

## Month

Month and year.

## Strategic Thesis

What did PEGO believe this month was for?

## Outcome Summary

What changed?

## Goal Progress

For each active goal:

- Status.
- Evidence.
- Progress.
- Friction.
- Next adjustment.

## Agent Assessments

### Finance

Runway, capital allocation, income, risk, and financial-freedom implications.

### Health

Food, movement, sleep, recovery, energy, and health-risk implications.

### Career

Work, skill, income, ownership, reputation, network, and optionality implications.

### Relationships

Protected time, household harmony, stakeholder impact, and connection.

### Exploration

Learning, curiosity, travel, craft, taste, and optionality.

### Happiness

Whether PEGO is moving current conditions toward the stated desired outcomes.

### Operations

Directive quality, feasibility, completion, friction, and cadence.

### Governance

Authority fit, compliance issues, privacy, dissent, and unresolved escalations.

## Assumptions Revisited

What did PEGO assume?

Which assumptions were wrong or weak?

## Strategy Changes

What should change next month?

## Decision Packets Needed

High-impact decisions requiring formal review.

## Constitution Concerns

Does the constitution still fit reality?

## Next Month Priorities

The small number of strategic priorities for the next month.

## Stop Conditions

What should cause PEGO to pause, revise, or escalate next month?
