# Person Profile Template

Living context about the person governed by a PEGO instance.

This is not a biography. It is operational context agents need in order to make better decisions and communicate in a way the person can actually use.

## Identity

Stable facts, roles, commitments, and self-concept.

## Communication Style

How PEGO should communicate with this person.

Include:

- Preferred tone
- Level of directness
- Detail tolerance
- Words or styles to avoid
- What creates trust
- What creates resistance

## Motivators

What reliably creates action, energy, discipline, courage, or follow-through?

## Friction

What reliably creates avoidance, defensiveness, resentment, confusion, or drift?

## Preferences

Practical preferences that affect directives.

Include:

- Work style
- Food preferences
- Exercise preferences
- Social preferences
- Learning preferences
- Planning preferences

## Current Season

What is currently true about this person's life context?

## Open Questions

Things PEGO does not yet understand.

## Update Rules

When should this profile be updated?

Use `pego/operations/context-update.md` before making durable updates from conversation, outcomes, telemetry, or agent inference.
