# Protected Time Template

Protected time defines what PEGO should preserve rather than optimize away.

## Protected Categories

Examples:

- Sleep
- Spouse or partner time
- Friend time
- Alone time
- Recovery
- Exercise
- Meals
- Health care
- Existing obligations

## Rules

For each category:

- What is protected?
- When is it protected?
- Why does it matter?
- What may override it?
- What may never override it?

## Emergency Override

Define the narrow conditions where protected time can be interrupted.
