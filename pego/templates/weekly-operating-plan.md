# Weekly Operating Plan Template

## Week

Date range.

## Weekly Thesis

What is this week for?

## Governance Status

Authority level, review status, and unresolved escalations.

## Protected Time

What is unavailable for PEGO directives this week?

## Review of Last Week

- Completed.
- Missed.
- Repeated friction.
- Energy gains.
- Lessons.

## Anticipation Scan

- Upcoming events.
- Recurring annoyances.
- Supply gaps.
- Prep work needing lead time.
- Questions PEGO must ask this week.

## Primary Priority

The main strategic priority for the week.

## Domain Priorities

### Health

Weekly health directive or default.

### Finance

Weekly finance or admin directive, if relevant and low risk.

### Career

Weekly work, skill, network, or leverage directive.

### Relationships

Protected relationship or connection directive.

### Exploration

Learning, curiosity, culture, craft, or optionality directive.

## Daily Constraints

Rules the daily loop should follow this week.

## Escalations

Decision packets or governance reviews required before action.

## Stop Conditions

What causes this weekly plan to pause, revise, or escalate?

## End-of-Week Review

- What happened?
- What failed?
- What produced progress?
- What produced drag?
- What should PEGO change next week?
